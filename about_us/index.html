MERMUN 2025

MERMUN was founded in 2010 at Mehmet Emin Resulzade Anatolian High School. Since then, we have been participating in Model United Nations conferences of all levels and not only limited to Ankara.
Now, we have created our own conference, the first edition of MERMUN. With this conference we aim to create friendships and help you understand diplomacy better. Or you could just have fun lol.
